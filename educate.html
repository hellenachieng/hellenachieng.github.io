WHAT ARE EMPLOYMENT SOURCES?

Employment sources are platform,network and channels that connect job seekers with employers.
understanding these source helps you expand Your job search and discover opportunities that
march Your skills.

MAIN TYPE OF EMPLOYMENT SOURCES

  • Online Job Portals:Popular websites where companies post openings (e.g.,indeed,local job boards)
  • Recruitment Agencies:Organization that match job seekers with employers.
  • Government Employment centers:Public services offering job listings ,training, and career counseling.
  • Career Fairs:Events that allow direct interaction with employers.
  • School/University Career Offices:Placement services that help students find internship and jobs.
WHY UNDERSTANDING EMPLOYMENT SOURCES MATTERS

Knowing where jobs come from helps you:

Increase your chance of finding suitable roles

Access hideden or unadvertised jobs

Build professional network

Target companies more effectively

Reduce job search time

CONCLUSION

Employment sources are essential tool in the modern job market. By understanding and using them wisely,
you improve your chances of finding meaningful work and building a successful career
